Lorenzo Alexander Chambers

Lorenzo Alexander Chambers is the author of three books. His first book, *The Principal: School Leadership in Real Time*, was a non-fiction first-person account of what it's like being a new principal in an elementary school in New York City. His second book was a screenplay titled *Desiderata*. Most recently, in December 2019, Lorenzo debuted his first novel, *Gypsy Woman!*

Lorenzo has over twenty years of experience as an educator from classroom teacher to the last sixteen years as an elementary school principal in Brooklyn, New York. Among his significant previous positions, Lorenzo lived in Italy as a professional football player for Giorgio Armani's now-defunct Milano Seamen, served as a licensed and registered representative, managed a firm trading account in equities, worked as an associate director of philanthropy at Dartmouth College, and taught as an adjunct professor at Long Island University School of Education. Lorenzo holds a Bachelor of Arts and a Master of Education degree from Long Island University, a degree from Dartmouth College, and advanced leadership certificates from Brooklyn College and the New York City Leadership Academy.

About Lorenzo Alexander Chambers

Lorenzo Alexander Chambers is the author of three books. His first book, *The Principal: School Leadership in Real Time*, was a non-fiction first-person account of what it's like being a new principal in an elementary school in New York City. His second book was a screenplay titled *Desiderata*. Most recently, in December 2019, Lorenzo debuted his first novel, *Gypsy Woman!*

Lorenzo has over twenty years of experience as an educator, from classroom teacher to the last sixteen years as an elementary school principal in Brooklyn, New York. Among his significant previous positions, Lorenzo lived in Italy as a professional football player, served as a licensed representative, managed a trading account in equities, worked as an associate director of philanthropy at Dartmouth College, and taught as an adjunct professor at Long Island University. Lorenzo holds multiple advanced degrees and leadership certificates.
